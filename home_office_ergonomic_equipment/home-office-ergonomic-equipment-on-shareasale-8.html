Creating Your Ergonomic Workspace

Once you've selected your equipment, proper setup is crucial:

  • Position your monitor at eye level, about an arm's length away
  • Adjust your chair so your feet rest flat on the floor
  • Keep frequently used items within easy reach
  • Ensure proper lighting to reduce eye strain
